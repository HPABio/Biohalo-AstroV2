# High-Level Summary

## Problem

Marine biofouling on ship hulls and offshore structures leads to:

* Increased drag
* Higher fuel consumption
* Higher emissions
* Increased maintenance costs

At the same time:

* Existing coatings rely on **biocides or PFAS**
* These face increasing **regulatory and sustainability pressure**

## Solution

BioHalo develops **biotechnologically produced mono-fluorinated compound (2-FMA)** as antifouling ingredients that are:

* Low fluorine
* Non-biocidal
* Durable antifouling
* Compatible with standard binder systems
* Aligned with EU and IMO regulations

# The Problem of Marine Fouling

### Marine Fouling Process

* Starts with microbial biofilm formation
* Progresses to colonization by barnacles and mussels

### Impact on Vessel Performance

* Hydrodynamic drag increases
* Fuel consumption increases **up to 40%**
* Greenhouse gas emissions increase

### Environmental & Economic Effects

* Spread of invasive species
* Ecosystem damage
* Accelerated corrosion
* Increased maintenance costs

### Role of Antifouling Coatings

Antifouling coatings:

* Improve efficiency
* Reduce emissions
* Lower operational costs
